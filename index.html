    History

    Vaels Educational Trust was established in 1992, by Dr. Ishari K Ganesh, M.Com, MBA, B.L, (M.L), Ph.D. a visionary and an eminent educationist. He founded this trust in the fond memory of his beloved father Shri. Ishari Velan, former minister of Tamil Nadu Government. The core mission of Vaels Educational Trust is to open new avenues of learning in order to empower the individuals and help them in becoming self- reliant.

    The University

    The Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India (GOI) has honored the Vels Group of Institutions with University status. The university is supported by VAELS trust, with the primary objective of transforming an individual into versatile professional and accomplished human being by developing the necessary Knowledge Skills and Attitude (KSA) and also to imbibe the passion to work towards national service and development. University is driven by an experienced and qualified team of board members. The Board of Management comprises of members from University Grants Commission (UGC) and various fields of knowledge and institutions. This includes members from different industries and sectors, eminent persons outside Academia, senior officials and renowned Academicians. The board meets periodically to frame policies and programmes for the growth and improvement of VISTAS under the leadership of the Vice-Chancellor.

    Vision

    To build the Institute as an epitome of excellence in higher education by effectively providing standard education and meticulous training for students to make them excel in their profession for betterment of the society.

    Mission

    • Effectively imparting knowledge and inculcating innovative thinking.
    • Facilitating skill enhancement through training and add-on courses.
    • Authentic research on contemporary industrial technique.
    • Facilitating appropriate co-curricular, extracurricular and extension activities.

    Chairman's Message

    Vels Institute of Science, Technology & Advanced Studies (VISTAS) also known as Vels University has successfully advanced into the thirteenth year since it was announced and considered to be a university in 2008. Rising from a modest start in 1992, Vels Group of Institutions has now expanded over 11 excellent educational campuses. These are equipped with international standard facilities, qualified faculty members, well trained staff, modem labs and equipment. The university also offers global exchange programs with international universities. We have a dedicated placement cell and our students have been successfully placed in top corporate companies and organizations. VISTAS holds a reputable status as an epitome of knowledge and research amongst the list of universities in India. It is admittedly a matter of great accomplishment for me to record these notable advancements that have been made in the field of giving higher education at the university The University has introduced many good practices for healthy and disciplined living, which include maintaining a healthy lifestyle, regular exercise, a healthy diet, and self-denial from tobacco and other harmful narcotics. Developing good habits during the educational period will pay huge returns later in life in the form of a healthy body and mind. We expect students to appreciate others, dress properly for the occasions, take on accountability, be persuasive, and adhere to university procedures. Innovative experimentation in many new disciplines and new scientific subjects have been introduced which will considerably help to build an atmosphere crucial for procuring a high academic standard
